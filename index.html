How it works

Three steps from surplus to sold

  1. 01

    List or browse

    Sellers post surplus stock with photos, specs and location. Buyers search by category, volume and state.

  2. 02

    Sort the deal

    Enquire, negotiate and confirm freight with the other business directly. Pay securely through Stripe.

  3. 03

    Move the stock

    Arrange pickup or delivery. Sellers clear space and recover cash, buyers get what they need for less.
