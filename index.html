How it works

  1. 1

    List and browse

    Businesses list surplus inventory while buyers browse available stock by category, volume and location.

    • Sellers clear space and expose stock quickly
    • Buyers access lower-cost alternatives in one place
    • Both sides work within one verified B2B marketplace
  2. 2

    Transact and coordinate delivery

    Matched businesses progress through a structured transaction path with clear handoff and delivery coordination.

    • Less back-and-forth between teams
    • Clear commercial process from enquiry to fulfilment
    • Reduced procurement and disposal friction
  3. 3

    Capture the outcome

    Both sides gain measurable value through lower costs, recovered revenue and improved circular outcomes.

    • Sellers reduce disposal costs and free up yard space
    • Buyers secure lower pricing on usable materials
    • Both reduce landfill and improve sustainability performance
